## About Our University

**A Few Words About the University**

Aman College of Science and Technology (ACST) is an Un aided Self Financing College Affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala. The Government of Kerala and MG University accorded their sanction to Aman Educational and Charitable Trust No.137/IV/ 2012.

The college has started its functioning in the year of 2013 and is located Just 7 KMs from Changanacherry and 4 KMs from Thiruvalla Town and 0.5 Kms from Paippad town, beside the old heritage "Thottayikkadavu", the ancient stream for ferry service between Alappuzha and hills and valleys of Thiruvalla. It is located in the heart of Paippad Town having enough and more conveyance facilities to reach the college. The college has a well arranged class rooms, lab and library facilities and smart class room. To students will get ample opportunity for physical activities through its football, volleyball, and badminton court. The college has an NSS unit, ED club and Placement cell for students social, entrepreneurial and career oriented growth.

The Management is committed to motivate the intellectual enlightenment and social advancement for younger generation in and around its catchment area. Aman College is a Dream Come true by a great visionary and educationist Haji P A Ummerkutty. At Present College has 6 UG courses and two PG courses affiliated to MG University. BBA and BCA course are approved by AICTE and affiliated to MG University.

The College prides itself on fostering a vibrant campus culture that encourages academic excellence and personal growth. Conferences, seminars and cultural events are organized on a regular basis to enhance the learning experience of students and provide them with a platform to showcase their talents The Institute places great emphasis on holistic teaching, ensuring that students are prepared better to meet the challenges of the business world and also responsible citizens. A dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art programs and a supportive learning environment make Aman College of Science and Technology ideal for those who want a well-rounded education.

## Management

Aman College of Science and Technology, Paippad is Established in the year of 2013 by Aman Educational and Charitable Trust organized by Educationalists, Philanthropists and Businessmen in and around paippad. It is a charitable and Non-profit organization which aims to provide maximum help to the needy in educational and other humanitarian fields. The members of the trust actually aim to uplist the down trodden, neglected and deserving people in the society. Through this vision the trust aims for the social development of the society.

The college is an exemplary model in the field of education by giving concession to economically weaker section. They not only provide fee concession but also give time relaxation for paying fee in installment basis.
