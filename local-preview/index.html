About Us

Aman College of Science and Technology (ACST) is an Un aided Self Financing College Affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala. The Government of Kerala and MG University accorded their sanction to Aman Educational and Charitable Trust No.137/IV/ 2012.

The college has started its functioning in the year of 2013 and is located Just 7 KMs from Changanacherry and 4 KMs from Thiruvalla Town and 0.5 Kms from Paippad town, beside the old heritage "Thottayikkadavu". It is located in the heart of Paippad Town with ample conveyance facilities. The college has well arranged class rooms, lab and library facilities and smart class room. Students get ample opportunity for physical activities through its football, volleyball, and badminton court. The college has an NSS unit, ED club and Placement cell for students' social, entrepreneurial and career oriented growth.
